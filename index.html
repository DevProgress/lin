About the artist:
Maya Lin is the designer behind some of the best-known architectural and land art displays of the 20th century, including the Vietnam Veterans Memorial. Her installations are inspired by landscapes and how individuals interact with terrain and their surrounding environment. She is currently working on what is her final memorial, “What is Missing?,” aimed at raising awareness about the biodiversity and habitat loss brought on by the current climate crisis.

About the project:
As Hillary Clinton put it, "climate change isn’t some abstract future threat—it threatens our families and economy right now." Maya Lin sought to highlight the urgent threat of climate change, mapping the impact extreme weather events and natural disasters that are growing more frequent and intense each year. (Sources for her data include the U.S. Department of Defense, NASA, the General Bathymetric Chart of the Oceans, the World Resources Institute, and the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.
